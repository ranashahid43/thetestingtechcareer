Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 23, 2025

The Testing Tech ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website (newcareer.vercel.app), submit a contact form, or apply for a position (spontaneous application).

1. Data We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal data:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number (from contact form).
  • Application Data: Name, email, phone, CV/resume, cover letter, portfolio, video intro (from spontaneous application form).
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited (via cookies/analytics).

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data only for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries or contact requests.
  • To evaluate your spontaneous job application and contact you if a suitable role becomes available.
  • To improve our website and services.
  • To comply with legal obligations (e.g., GDPR).

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your data based on:

  • Your consent (checkbox in forms).
  • Contractual necessity (to process your application or respond to inquiries).
  • Legitimate interests (improving our website, fraud prevention).

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share it with:

  • Service providers (e.g., hosting, email service) under strict confidentiality.
  • Legal authorities if required by law.

5. Data Retention

We keep your data only as long as necessary:

  • Contact form inquiries: up to 12 months.
  • Spontaneous applications: up to 24 months (or until you request deletion).

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Erase your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restrict processing
  • Object to processing
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@thetestingtech.com.

7. Security

We use appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

8. Cookies & Analytics

We may use cookies for analytics and site performance. You can manage cookies via your browser settings.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here with the updated date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us:

Email: info@thetestingtech.com

Address: Via della Concordia, Italy
